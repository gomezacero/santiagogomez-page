## Context

Independent accountants in Colombia are trapped in operational work: up to 80 hours a month just typing in invoices, 14-hour days during tax closings and 85% turning down new clients for lack of capacity. Contabio exists to break that ceiling.

## Problem

The work that burns accountants out isn't analysis — it's data entry, repetitive payroll runs and the fear of missing a DIAN deadline (penalties for errors in exogenous information average millions of pesos). All of that is automatable.

## Solution

- **Goodbye data entry**: the accountant uploads photos, PDFs or Excel files; the AI detects every field, structures the information and delivers ready-to-download files in seconds.
- **Smart payroll**: automatic runs and validations integrating every legal parameter, with regulatory compliance in each calculation.
- **Tax peace of mind**: per-client smart calendars with proactive alerts well ahead of every obligation.
- **Exogenous-information auditor** (in development): analysis of massive datasets to catch discrepancies and inconsistencies the human eye misses.

## Results

**Live in production** at [contabio.pro](https://contabio.pro), in launch phase with an open beta. The product's promise is its own headline: multiply your clients without multiplying your working hours.
