## Context

Managing a small brand's social media eats hours: deciding what to post, producing the assets and publishing them at the right time on each network. Fanpage AI Manager automates that whole cycle.

## Problem

Generic AI produces generic content. For posts to sound like the brand, the system needs to understand its identity — tone, colors, audience — and keep it consistent across every network and language.

## Solution

- **Brand profiles**: each fanpage is configured with its branding and identity; it can even be seeded from the brand's existing PDF documents.
- **AI generation**: Gemini and OpenAI produce content ideas and media assets consistent with the brand profile.
- **Scheduled publishing** to Facebook, Instagram and TikTok with a draggable calendar (dnd-kit).
- **Solid foundation**: Supabase with SSR for auth and data, Zod-validated forms, transactional email with Resend and internationalization with next-intl.

## Results

A workflow where the brand defines its identity once and the system proposes, produces and schedules the content — the human just curates and approves.
