## Context

The "polla" is a Latin American World Cup tradition: predict the scores and compete with friends. PollaValeum takes it global for the 2026 World Cup — anyone predicts all 104 matches and competes on a general leaderboard.

## Problem

Two things break a prediction league: someone copying other people's picks, and a corrected result forcing manual point recalculation. Both were solved in the database, not the frontend.

## Solution

- **Anti-copy with Row Level Security**: other players' predictions are invisible at the Postgres level until kickoff; the lock is validated against the server clock.
- **Scoring via idempotent SQL triggers**: loading or correcting a score automatically recalculates every player's points. Zero manual maintenance during the tournament.
- **A design with identity**: a fusion of sports-broadcast graphics (night blue + gold, scoreboards) and a retro sticker album — every prediction is a collectible card.

## Results

An app ready to run the whole tournament with a single administrator: load results and assign knockout brackets; everything else derives itself.
